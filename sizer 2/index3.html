How to Measure Your Ring Size

Follow these steps to measure your ring size:

  1. Wrap a piece of string or paper around the base of your finger.
  2. Mark the point where the two ends meet.
  3. Measure the length in millimeters using a ruler.
  4. Refer to our sizing chart to find your ring size.
